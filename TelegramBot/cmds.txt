<b><u>YLD Info:</u></b>
<b>/price</b> - Current price
<b>/supply</b> - Current supply and circulation
<b>/mcap</b> - Current market cap and 24h volume
<b>/holders</b> - Get the current total number of holders

<b><u>Top Holders:</u></b>
<b>/top</b> - Displays the top 10 holders, or provide a number (i.e. "100" would get the top 100 holders)
<b>/rtop</b> - Displays the top 10 RFI-YLD liquidity provider holders in the garden, or provide a number (i.e. "100" would get the top 100 RFI-YLD LP holders)
<b>/etop</b> - Displays the top 10 ETH-YLD liquidity provider holders in the garden, or provide a number (i.e. "100" would get the top 100 ETH-YLD LP holders)

<b><u>Share Amounts:</u></b>
<b>/share</b> - Provide a number of YLD (i.e. "350"), get the share percentage. Provide a percentage (i.e. "0.35%"), get the number of YLD
<b>/rshare</b> - RFI liquidity provider in the garden: Provide a number of RFI-YLD (i.e. "5"), get the share percentage. Provide a percentage (i.e. "0.35%"), get the number of RFI-YLD
<b>/eshare</b> - ETH liquidity provider in the garden: Provide a number of ETH-YLD (i.e. "0.1"), get the share percentage. Provide a percentage (i.e. "0.35%"), get the number of ETH-YLD
